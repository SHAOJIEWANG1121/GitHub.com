Title: My Grandmother (Comics)
Medium: Screen Printing, Japanese Bookbinding
Size: 420mm*594mm
Date: 2017

This comics is one part of my personal project at London College of Communication, University of the Arts London. which based on my own story about my grandmother. She left about 12 years ago, I even do not remember what she looks like. The only thing she left us is endless sorrows.
