Title: Diego and Frida
Medium: Chinese Brush & Ink, Photoshop
Size: 420mm*594mm
Date: 2016

Diego and Frida both played important roles in Mexico literature history. In addition to their own art accomplishments, people are also quite delight to talk about their astonishing marriage. Diego’s womanising, Frida’s physical suffering, and psychological traumatise given by Diego made their marriage special.My main idea came from the masterpiece Diego and Frida written by Le Clézio, the winner of the Nobel Prize in Literature.
